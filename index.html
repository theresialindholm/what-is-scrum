What is SCRUM?

Scrum is one of several techniques for managing product development. It falls under the broad category of Agile Software Development.
The word scrum origins from the term, borrowed from rugby, where a scrum is a formation of players.

How good is scrum really, does it bring a smooth and effective work flow or is scrum making you a worse engingeer? Take a listen to the Stack Overflow podcast where they dive in to the scrum methodology.
